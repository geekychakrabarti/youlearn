Getting Started

Create a playlist with the + button, then add videos from Discover or paste a YouTube URL.

Library Search

Type in the search box to filter by title or semantic tags.

  • in:transcripts — search spoken content in transcripts
  • in:current — scope to the active playlist only
  • Combine: camera view in:transcripts in:current

Discover Search Operators

  • from:channel — videos from a specific creator
  • duration:short / medium / long — filter by length
  • order:views / order:recent — sort results

While Watching a Video

  • All tab — chapters, clips, notes and questions in time order. Click any item to jump to it.
  • Chapters tab — search transcripts with [ / ] to navigate matches.
  • ↩ Similar projects — finds YouTube videos on the same topic at similar length.
  • Study Sheet — exports your chapters, questions and notes with timestamps.

Tips

  • Semantic tags on video cards are clickable — use them to filter your library.
  • Click a playlist name twice to rename it. Use ▾ to collapse playlists.
  • Transcript search stays open so you can click multiple results without re-searching.
  • Sort your library by Newest, Title A→Z, or Duration ↑/↓ — click again to reverse.
